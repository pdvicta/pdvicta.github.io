About Me

Patricia Victa is a 21-year-old college student from the Philippines, an emerging writer and voracious reader on the side.
She writes under the pen name Viktoria Love, dabbling in poetry, short stories, and long-form fiction in the fantasy, mystery, adventure, and psychology genre.
Her work had been featured in Launchora's The Storyteller (Winter 2017, Spring 2018, and Fall 2018 edition) and her books can be accessed through Kindle Vella.
